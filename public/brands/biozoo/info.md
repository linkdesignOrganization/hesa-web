## Descripcion (ES)

Bio Zoo es un laboratorio farmaceutico veterinario mexicano fundado en 1955 por el Dr. Abelardo Martin Novelo en Guadalajara, Mexico. Se especializa en crear soluciones integradas para la salud animal, ofreciendo productos farmaceuticos para diversas especies incluyendo vacunas (Inmunovax), suplementos nutricionales (PetWellness) y productos lacteos para cachorros (GrowLac Pets). Su lema "Esta contigo" refleja su compromiso con los profesionales veterinarios.

## Descripcion (EN)

Bio Zoo is a Mexican veterinary pharmaceutical laboratory founded in 1955 by Dr. Abelardo Martin Novelo in Guadalajara, Mexico. The company specializes in creating integrated animal health solutions, offering pharmaceutical products for multiple species including vaccines (Inmunovax), nutritional supplements (PetWellness), and dairy supplements for puppies (GrowLac Pets). Operating across multiple Latin American countries, their tagline "Esta contigo" reflects their commitment to veterinary professionals.
