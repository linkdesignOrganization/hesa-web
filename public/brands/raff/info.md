## Descripcion (ES)

Raff es una marca italiana de alimento para aves ornamentales fabricada por Valpet, empresa ubicada en Granarolo dell'Emilia, Bologna, Italia. Ofrece diversos productos especializados para aves como alimento universal, pasta de huevo, Holland Rosso y otras formulas disenadas para promover energia, plumaje saludable y vitalidad en aves enjauladas. Sus productos son reconocidos en el mercado europeo y latinoamericano por su calidad y formulacion especializada.

## Descripcion (EN)

Raff is an Italian ornamental bird food brand manufactured by Valpet, a company located in Granarolo dell'Emilia, Bologna, Italy. It offers various specialized products for birds such as universal food, egg paste, Holland Rosso, and other formulas designed to promote energy, healthy plumage, and vitality in caged birds. Their products are recognized in the European and Latin American markets for their quality and specialized formulation.
