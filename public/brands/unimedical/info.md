## Descripcion (ES)

Laboratorio Unimedical es una empresa uruguaya lider en la produccion, comercializacion y exportacion de productos farmaceuticos veterinarios para mascotas y animales pequenos. Desarrolla formulaciones basadas en avances cientificos y estandares internacionales, ofreciendo medicamentos tanto para administracion parenteral como oral. Su lema es "Calidad con Seguridad" y exporta a multiples paises incluyendo Costa Rica a traves de Herrera y Elizondo S.A.

## Descripcion (EN)

Laboratorio Unimedical is a leading Uruguayan producer, marketer, and exporter of veterinary pharmaceutical products for companion animals and small animals. The company develops formulations based on scientific advances and international standards, offering both injectable and oral administration medications. Headquartered in Montevideo, they serve demanding international markets with products including antibiotics, anti-inflammatories, and antiparasitic medications.
