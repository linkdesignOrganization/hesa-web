## Descripcion (ES)

Mustad es una compania noruega fundada en 1832 en Gjovik, dedicada a la fabricacion de herraduras, clavos para herraje y equipos para el cuidado equino. Con mas de 190 anos de experiencia, Mustad Hoofcare Group es el mayor fabricante y distribuidor mundial de productos para el cuidado de cascos de caballos. Conocida como "The Hoofcare People", ofrece herraduras, clavos, raspas, herramientas para herradores y suplementos especializados.

## Descripcion (EN)

Mustad is a Norwegian company founded in 1832 in Gjovik, dedicated to manufacturing horseshoes, horseshoe nails, and equine hoof care equipment. With over 190 years of experience, Mustad Hoofcare Group is the world's largest manufacturer and distributor of horseshoe and farrier equipment. Known as "The Hoofcare People," they produce horseshoes, nails, rasps, farrier tools, and specialty hoof care products serving farriers, veterinarians, and horse owners globally.
