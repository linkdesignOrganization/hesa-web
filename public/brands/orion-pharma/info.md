## Descripcion (ES)

Orion Pharma Animal Health es la division veterinaria del Grupo Orion, una empresa farmaceutica finlandesa con mas de 100 anos de experiencia. Fabrica y comercializa medicamentos veterinarios propios, incluyendo productos especializados como Dexdomitor (dexmedetomidina) y Antisedan (atipamezol) para sedacion y anestesia en animales. Con un portafolio de mas de 600 productos, opera en seis continentes con presencia en multiples paises.

## Descripcion (EN)

Orion Pharma Animal Health is the veterinary division of the Orion Group, a Finnish pharmaceutical company with over 100 years of pharmaceutical expertise. The company manufactures and markets proprietary veterinary medicines including Dexdomitor (dexmedetomidine) and Antisedan (atipamezole) for animal sedation and anesthesia. With a portfolio of over 600 products, it operates across six continents with sales organizations in multiple countries.
