## Descripcion (ES)

New Born Animal Care es una empresa francesa especializada en nutraceuticos y alimentos funcionales de alta calidad para animales domesticos y ganaderia. La marca Raiz'Up ofrece productos energeticos como PuppyBoost, BoostyVo, HorsyBoost y PiggyBoost, disenados bajo regulaciones de la Union Europea. Su filosofia es "Boosters for life!", proporcionando suplementos nutricionales innovadores para optimizar la salud animal en sus primeras etapas de vida.

## Descripcion (EN)

New Born Animal Care is a French company specializing in high-quality nutraceuticals and functional feeds for companion animals and livestock. The Raiz'Up brand offers energetic products such as PuppyBoost, BoostyVo, HorsyBoost, and PiggyBoost, manufactured according to EU regulations. Their philosophy is "Boosters for life!", providing innovative nutritional supplements to optimize animal health in their early life stages.
